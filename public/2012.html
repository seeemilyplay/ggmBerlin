About

GeekGirlMeetup is free un-conference on web, start-up and code. It's a one day event, and the attendees are the programme. Everyone can contribute and volunteer to get involved!

From PageRank to DiscoRank: make your search results swing!
In this session we will describe the PageRank, a reranking algorithm invented by Larry Page which boosts popular items on top of your search results. This session is for all those with a curiosity for how search engines and ranking algorithms work. Not technical knowledge is needed as we will cover the theoretical foundations of the PageRank (such as the Random Walk and Markov Chains) with concrete examples. We will also explain how the PageRank is used in large search engines like Google's. We will finally present our own improved version of the PageRank, the DiscoRank, that supports universal search in the Next SoundCloud.
